Ad System Test Page

This page is used to verify the DeMARK ad system is working correctly end to end. Check the banner at the top, the sidebar ad on the right, and the fullscreen overlay on load.

✓ Banner Slot

Should appear above the header. Renders the banner_mobile or banner_desktop asset depending on screen size.

✓ Half Screen Slot

Should appear in the sidebar on the right. Renders half_mobile or half_desktop asset.

✓ Fullscreen Overlay

Should appear 1.5 seconds after page load as a fullscreen overlay with a close button. Only shows once per browser session.

✓ Click Tracking

Clicking any ad should open the destination URL in a new tab and log the click in your Supabase clicks table. Check Analytics in your dashboard after clicking.
